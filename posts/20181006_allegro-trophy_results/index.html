Allegro Tournament, 06 October 2018

Posted on October 6, 2018

The Allegro Tournament commenced on 14 September 2018 with nine games being played involving seven players. Games are usually played in pairs as players have to play each other twice, once with white and black. After four nights of play, 22 of the 42 games have been completed. The Fischer time control used is ten minutes plus ten seconds.
